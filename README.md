# Reverie House

**A home for all dreamers in our wild mindscape.**

## What is Reverie House?

**[Reverie House](https://reverie.house)** is a novel community and social experience for writers, artists, and dreamweavers of all sorts.

### Who made this?

*[Seeker's Reverie](https://www.amazon.ca/Seekers-Reverie-House-errantson-ebook/dp/B0F2ST7CXZ)* was written by **[errantson](https://www.amazon.ca/Seekers-Reverie-House-errantson-ebook/dp/B0F2ST7CXZ)** and released it to [CC0 (public domain)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

Since its release, others have begun contributing to the structures of **[Reverie House](https://reverie.house)** for the benefit of our wild mindscape:

### How do I enjoy this?

**1. Visit and Follow**

Start at **[reverie.house](https://reverie.house)** or follow **[@reverie.house](https://bsky.app/profile/reverie.house)** on Bluesky to begin your journey.

**2. Meet the Greeter**

**[Speak your name](https://bsky.app/profile/did:plc:yauphjufk7phkwurn266ybx2/post/3lljjzcydwc25)** with what you wish to be called in our community. Our current Greeter of Reveries will welcome you and provide you a `.reverie.house` handle to don.

**3. Declare Your Origin Dream**

[PLEASE TELL [@MAPPY.REVERIE.HOUSE](https://bsky.app/profile/mappy.reverie.house) TO FINISH WRITING THIS]

**4. Meet Other Dreamweavers**

Custom Feeds:
- **[Expanded Lore](https://bsky.app/profile/did:web:reverie.house/feed/expanded-lore)** — All posts labeled as canon in the living story
- **[Idle Dreaming](https://bsky.app/profile/did:web:reverie.house/feed/idle-dreaming)** — Every post from every dreamer in the community

Browse the **[dreamers list](https://reverie.house/dreamers.html)** and follow those whose dreams resonate with yours.

**6. Share Your Dreams**

Use the **[lore.farm](https://lore.farm)** integration to certify your posts are part of the shared story. Your labeled posts feed into the **[lore.farm](https://lore.farm)** stream, integrating your stories into the grander narrative. Participating this way will earn **[souvenirs](https://reverie.house/souvenirs.html)** throughout your journey.

**7. Read and Support**

Visit **[the library](https://reverie.house/library.html)** to read books online (free), download ePub/Kindle formats, or **[order physical copies](https://reverie.house/order.html)** for yourself. Request delivery to your favorite bookstores to support the community funds and improve our wild mindscape!
