What is Reverie House?

Reverie House is a shared creative mindscape built on the ATProtocol network (also known as Bluesky).

Think of it as a collaborative storytelling world where:

  • 🌌 Dreamers (that's you!) position themselves on a personality spectrum
  • 📜 Stories become part of our shared canon through lore.farm
  • 🎨 Souvenirs are collected as you explore and contribute
  • 🤝 Community is built through shared creativity and connection
2

What is ATProtocol?

ATProtocol (Authenticated Transfer Protocol) is the decentralized social network that powers Bluesky.

Unlike traditional social media, ATProtocol lets you:

  • 🏠 Own your identity (like @yourname.reverie.house)
  • 🔓 Take your data anywhere
  • 🌐 Connect across different servers and apps
  • 🛠️ Use custom labelers (like our lore.farm) to organize content
💡 Good to know: You don't need a Bluesky account to explore Reverie House, but you'll need one to become a resident and fully participate.

The Personality Spectrum

At Reverie House, every dreamer positions themselves on three axes:

Order ⟷ Chaos

Do you prefer structure or spontaneity?

Unity ⟷ Autonomy

Do you seek collective harmony or individual freedom?

Hedonism ⟷ Asceticism

Do you embrace pleasure or practice restraint?

These three dimensions create eight octants—archetypal personalities like "architect," "wanderer," "seeker," or "hermit."

Canon & lore.farm

Our stories live on lore.farm—a decentralized proof-of-lore system.

Here's how it works:

  1. 📝 Dreamers post stories about Reverie House on Bluesky
  2. 🏷️ Stories are tagged as "lore" using our labeler
  3. ⭐ Canon Keepers (trusted community members) review and canonize the best stories
  4. 📚 Canon stories become the official shared history of Reverie House
💡 What's a labeler? It's an ATProtocol feature that lets communities tag and organize content. Our lore.farm labeler helps us track which stories are part of our canon.

Becoming a Resident

To fully participate in Reverie House, you'll need to create an account on our ATProtocol server.

This gives you:

  • 🏠 A unique handle like @yourname.reverie.house
  • 🎨 The ability to set your spectrum position
  • 📜 The ability to share stories and contribute to canon
  • 🎁 The ability to collect souvenirs
  • 🤝 Full participation in our community
⚠️ Currently invite-only: Reverie House is still in beta. You'll need an invite code to create an account.
